Unstoppable Siren

Instantly forces device volume to 100%. If a thief tries to lower it, LCLD pulls it back to maximum instantly. It cannot be stopped without unlocking the device.

🛑

Aggressive Theft Mode

A multi-action protocol that locks the phone, starts aggressive GPS reporting, and triggers the siren simultaneously. It resumes instantly even if the device is rebooted.

📡

Offline SMS Commands

Complete control via simple SMS texts secured by a 6-digit PIN. Trigger cameras, wipe data, or locate the device without needing 4G or WiFi connection.

📸

Silent Remote Capture

Silently triggers the front or rear hardware cameras and uploads the evidence securely, without alerting the person holding the device.
